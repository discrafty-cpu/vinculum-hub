📖 Vocabulary

Picture Graph A graph that uses pictures or symbols to show data.
Data Information we collect, like counts or preferences.
Key Information showing what each symbol or picture means. Example: "Each apple = 1 vote."
Category A group or type of thing. In graphs: apples, oranges, and bananas are categories of fruit.
Compare Look at two amounts to see which is more, less, or if they are equal.

🔗 About Graphs

A picture graph is like a mapping system: each category (like a type of fruit) connects to a count (like how many votes it got). This is one of the first ways we represent relationships in math—a function that maps categories to quantities.
