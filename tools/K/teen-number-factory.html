Watch cubes roll down the conveyor belt. Tap them to collect them. When you have 10, bundle them into a ten-stick to see how teen numbers are made!
Key Idea
Teen numbers are made of 1 ten and some ones. 10 + 3 = 13. The tens place and ones place work together!
