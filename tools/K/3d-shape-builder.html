Key Idea
3D shapes are made from flat 2D faces connected by edges at vertices. By assembling faces, we understand how 3D shapes are built!
Vocabulary
Face
A flat surface of a 3D shape
Edge
A line where two faces meet
Vertex
A corner where edges meet
