Solving Linear Equations

Use inverse operations to isolate the variable. What you do to one side, you must do to the other.

Example: 2x + 5 = 13

Step 1: Subtract 5 from both sides
2x + 5 - 5 = 13 - 5
2x = 8

Step 2: Divide both sides by 2
2x ÷ 2 = 8 ÷ 2
x = 4

Check: 2(4) + 5 = 8 + 5 = 13 ✓
