📚 Vocabulary
Tens
A group of 10 ones. A rod in base-ten blocks.
Ones
Single units. Small squares in base-ten blocks.
Digit
A symbol (0–9) used to write numbers.
Place Value
The value of a digit based on its position.
Regroup
Trade 10 ones for 1 ten, or 1 ten for 10 ones.
