Vocabulary
Tens A group of 10 ones. We use a long rod to show 10.
Ones Single units. Each small square = 1.
Regroup When 10 ones combine to make 1 ten, or breaking a ten into 10 ones.
Carry When we regroup ones into a ten during addition.
Borrow When we break a ten into ones during subtraction.
Tips

• Align numbers by tens and ones place

• Always start with the ones place

• Watch for regrouping moments

• Double-check your carry marks
