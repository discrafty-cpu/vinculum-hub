How It Works
Multiplicative comparison uses language like "times as many" or "times as much" to compare two quantities. One quantity is a multiple of the other.
Cultural Context
This tool includes word problems from Anishinaabe, Dakota, and other Indigenous contexts showing real-world uses of multiplicative comparison in harvesting and trading.
