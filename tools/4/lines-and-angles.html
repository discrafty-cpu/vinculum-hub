INDIGENOUS: Yup'ik context

Fish Rack Construction: In building a traditional Yup'ik fish drying rack, vertical posts must be perpendicular to the ground (at a 90° angle), and horizontal bars must be parallel to each other. These geometric relationships ensure the structure is stable and functional. Understanding geometric precision connects to traditional building practices.
