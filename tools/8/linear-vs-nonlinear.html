Linear vs Nonlinear Functions

Linear: Straight line, constant rate of change, equation of form y = mx + b

Nonlinear: Curved, rate of change is not constant, includes quadratics, exponentials, etc.

Linear: y = 2x + 1

Nonlinear: y = x²

Characteristics

Linear Nonlinear
Straight line Curved
Constant slope Changing slope
Degree 1 equation Degree 2 or higher
