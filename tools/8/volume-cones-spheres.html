Volume: Cones, Cylinders, Spheres

8.G.9

Volume Formulas

Cylinder

V = πr²h

r = radius, h = height

Cone

V = (1/3)πr²h

r = radius, h = height

Sphere

V = (4/3)πr³

r = radius
